Tech pivot

Our tech team discovered that writing information to NOMIS carried a large risk. As such, it was decided that inputting data would be removed from the scope of our service. Our service would calculate release dates based on information in NOMIS. This changed the overall journey and the hypotheses we would test.

  1. WE BELIEVE

    IF we show the user everything currently inputted into NOMIS to review before the calculation is done

    FOR all users with a calculator mindset

    THEN the user can feel confident we have taken the specific information from NOMIS into account with the calculation

    AND gives them the opportunity to spot a mistake to fix in NOMIS before the calculation is done

    We would know this is true when

    • We understand how users feel about adapting any mistakes on NOMIS and returning to our service
    • We understand if users having to pick closest to in NOMIS impacts their sense of trust for our service
    • Users feel we have captured all necessary information from NOMIS to complete the calculation

  2. WE BELIEVE

    IF we show a full sentence calculation breakdown at the end of the journey

    FOR all members of staff

    THEN this will help them trust the service

    We would know this is true when staff accept dates calculated by the service
