Initial prototype

What we did

  • Produced a prototype in Balsamiq (as an interaction designer had not yet joined the team)
  • Initial hypotheses were defined and prioritised in various workshops
  • A journey was mapped and defined based on our discovery research
  • The prototype reflected this journey and otherwise followed the GOV.UK design system and content design guidelines
  • The order of the design approximated the order of the paper calculation sheet

Our core hypotheses

  1. WE BELIEVE

    IF we show a full sentence calculation breakdown at the end of the journey

    FOR all members of staff

    THEN this will help them trust the service

    We would know this is true when staff accept dates calculated by the service

  2. WE BELIEVE

    IF we create a mandatory list of questions

    FOR all members of staff

    THEN we know they are considering all necessary information

    We would know this is true when when experienced members of staff feel everything necessary has been considered
