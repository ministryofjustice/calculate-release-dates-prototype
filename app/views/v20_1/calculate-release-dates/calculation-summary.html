Why are some details missing?
Some dates and details are not included as according to PSI 03/2015 (opens in new tab) they are not relevant to this person's circumstances.
How are final release dates calculated?
Some calculations are made up of different sentences and terms, such as overlapping or concurrent sentences. The final release date comes from the sentence or term with the latest release date.
