Sentence card

Component Stable

Display sentence and offence information.

What this component does

Use the sentence card to present a personâ€™s sentence and offence information.

The layout of the sentence card mirrors the use of index cards or paper in the real world. By separating individual sentences into separate cards, information specific to that sentence, such as the offence, sentence length or sentence type can be clearly seen in proximity to the sentence itself.

Separation between sentence cards makes information easier to read, and eliminates the visual clutter that can occur when presenting information in a table. Because the sentence card uses rows instead of columns, they are scalable when showing more complex sentence information.
