What this page does

This page shows users the final release dates that have been calculated by the service.

Users are shown the release dates themselves, and a breakdown of how the service reached these dates. Because trust was a key user need of the service, requiring confidence in the calculation result by the user, the service has been designed to be transparent.

The Calculation breakdown clearly shows each stage of the calculation. This has been designed to reflect a manual calculation and to have familiarity, similar to an itemised phone bill or tax document.
