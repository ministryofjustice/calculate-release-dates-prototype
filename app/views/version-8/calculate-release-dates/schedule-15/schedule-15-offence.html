Schedule 15 offences cannot yet be identified by this service. To make sure release dates are calculated correctly, you must select sentences that include a Schedule 15 offence.

How are release date calculations affected?

In some cases, a person's release dates are calculated at two thirds of their sentence in custody. This happens when the person:

  • is sentenced for a Schedule 15 offence that may attract a maximum of life imprisonment
  • is 18 years or over
  • is sentenced on or after 1 April 2020
  • has received 7 years or more from a single Standard Determinate Sentence or Detention in a Young Offender Institution

Lists of Schedule 15 offences

There are 3 lists of Schedule 15 offences. The lists are grouped by sentence date and sentence length.

Sentence dates from 1 April 2020 and 27 June 2022

  • Sentences over 7 years

Sentence dates after 28 June 2022

  • Sentences between 4 and 7 years
  • Sentences over 7 years
