Some sentences could have alternative release arrangements

This service has identified that some sentences in this calculation could be affected by:

  • The Release of Prisoners (Alteration of Relevant Proportion of Sentence) Order 2020 (SDS+)
  • Police, Crime, Sentencing and Courts Act 2022

Where an offence can attract a maximum life sentence, the release dates for some sentences are calculated at two thirds. This is also known as an SDS+ arrangement.

What are the criteria for an SDS+ release arrangement?

SDS+ release arrangements can depend on the:

  • offence
  • sentence type
  • sentence length
  • sentence date
  • age of the person at the point of sentence

What you need to do

On the following 4 pages, you must select the Schedule 15 offences with SDS+ release arrangements, from the relevant lists.

List A

The offences in list A are applicable to:

  • SDS, DYOI and SEC250 sentence types
