This calculation needs more information

Some sentences in this calculation have offences that could change the way it is calculated. These sentences can have release dates that are calculated at two thirds (also known as an SDS+, which is completed on a peach calculation sheet).

What are the criteria for an SDS+?

SDS+ release arrangements can depend on the:

  • offence
  • sentence type
  • sentence length
  • sentence date
  • age of the person when the sentence was imposed

What you need do

This service has identified sentences that meet the criteria for SDS+ release arrangements. These have been highlighted on the following pages.

Some of the offences for these sentences could be Schedule 15 offences. You must identify those that are by looking them up on a list.
