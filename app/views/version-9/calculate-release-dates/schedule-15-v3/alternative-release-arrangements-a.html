Some sentences in this calculation meet the criteria for SDS+ release arrangements specified by List A.

The offences that appear in List A are applicable to:

  • SDS, DYOI, SEC91 and SEC250 sentence types
  • sentences imposed on or after 01 April 2020
  • sentences imposed before 28 June 2022
  • individual sentences of 7 years or over

  • a maximum sentence of life

What you need to do

For each sentence, select the offences that have alternative release date arrangements.

  1. Look up the name of the offence for each sentence on List A (opens in new tab)
  2. Select those sentences that have offences that appear on List A
Wording of incohate offences

Some incohate offences might include wording that do not appear as named sentences on the list, such as:

  • aiding, abbetting councelling or procuring
  • attempted or attempt to
  • conspiriciy to
  • incite or incitement
  • where the person intended or believed would commit an offence

Such offences could still be specified Schedule 15 offences. See paragraphs 64 (specified violent offences) and paragraph 153 (specified sexual offences) for more information.
