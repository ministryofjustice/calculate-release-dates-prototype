Report a problem with the calculation

You can report a problem with the Calculate release dates service (CRDS) calculation if:

  • sentence and offence information from the warrants and CRDS does not match
  • you think the dates calculated by the service are incorrect
  • the calculation includes terrorism or terrorism-related offences

To report a problem

  1. Open a new email addressed to specialistsupportteam@digital.justice.gov.uk.
  2. Enter the subject line: Ref f5938-g4930-r5745-f4958-g3294.
  3. Explain the problem in the email and attach the relevant court documents.
  4. Specify if you need APD, HDCAD, ROTL or ERSED included.
  5. Send the email to the specialist support team. They should respond within 2 working days.
